About the Tutor

Adil Nutkani is a dedicated and passionate educator with over 12 years of hands-on teaching experience in Physics and Mathematics. Specializing in 9th and 10th grade curriculum, he has helped hundreds of students transform their academic performance and build lasting confidence in STEM subjects.

Students appreciate his patience, clarity, and genuine investment in their success. Many former students credit Adil with igniting their love for science and mathematics.
